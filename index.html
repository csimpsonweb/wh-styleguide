Brand Colors

Our primary palette is confident, masculine and informed. It takes care of the more ‘formal’ side of things and provides us with a striking identity to base our brand on. A tone of charcoal grey helps us maintain contrast whilst avoiding a cold and unapproachable look.

Headings

All headings have a default margin bottom of 0.5rem and a font weight of bold. The Heading font size is equal to the computated font size of the root element, font sizes at the different breakpoints will need to be considered
